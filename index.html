Send & Receive Coins

Once your Trezor device is set up with Trezor Suite, you can send and receive cryptocurrencies with ease. Track Your Portfolio

With Trezor Suite, managing and tracking your crypto portfolio is a breeze. View your holdings, monitor market trends, and stay up-to-date with real-time price updates. The app provides a clear overview of your assets, helping you make informed decisions about your investments.

Trade Crypto Assets

Looking to trade or swap your coins? Trezor Suite allows you to manage your trades seamlessly. Connect your wallet to various supported exchanges and execute transactions directly from the app. Every trade or asset transfer requires confirmation on your Trezor device, ensuring that your crypto remains secure at all times.

Crypto isn’t just for investments. With Trezor Suite, you can also use your coins to pay for daily essentials. From groceries to online shopping, you can securely make payments using supported cryptocurrencies—no need for third-party services or complex processes.
